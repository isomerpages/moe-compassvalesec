<p><strong><u>MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS)</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Eligible Singapore Citizens (SC) can receive financial assistance from
MOE on fees and other school expenses. All students applying for FAS must
submit the MOE FAS application online regardless of which FAS scheme they
are applying for. Students who do not qualify for MOE FAS will be considered
for School-Based FAS (SBFAS).</p>
<p><strong><u>Schemes Available</u></strong>
</p>
<p>There are 2 types of financial assistance schemes (FAS):</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Ministry of Education FAS (MOE FAS)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>School-Based FAS (SBFAS) Category A and Category B
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>1. <u>Ministry of Education FAS (MOE FAS) Income Eligibility</u> (click dropdown arrow below to reveal)</strong>
</p>
<p>Income Eligibility Criteria</p>
<p>Gross Monthly Household Income (GHI) does not exceed:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>i. $4,000 or</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ii. $1,000 per capita</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;
<br>Benefits</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>i. 100% subsidy on school and miscellaneous fees</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ii. Free textbooks and school attire</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>iii. School meal subsidy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>iv. Public transport subsidy</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>2. <u>School-Based FAS (SBFAS) Income Eligibility Criteria and Benefits</u> </strong>
<br><strong>(click dropdown arrow below to reveal)</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>SBFAS Category A</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Income Eligibility Criteria
<br>Gross Monthly Household Income (GHI) does not exceed:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>i. $4,500 or</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ii.$1,125 per capita income</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;Benefits</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>i. 100% subsidy on school and miscellaneous fees</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ii. Free textbooks and 2 sets of PE attire</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>iv. School meal subsidy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>v.&nbsp; Public transport subsidy</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>SBFAS Category B</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Income Eligibility Criteria
<br>Gross Monthly Household Income (GHI) does not exceed:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>i. $5,500 or</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ii.$1,375 per capita income</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;Benefits</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>i. 100% subsidy on school and miscellaneous fees</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ii. Free textbooks</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Important Notes</u></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>For students with approved FAS application, they will not have to pay
for their school uniforms and textbooks purchased at the bookshop. Hence,
students should not purchase these items from the bookshop till they have
received approval of their FAS application.&nbsp; There will not be any
reimbursement or refunds for any items purchased prior to approval of their
application.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may refer to <strong><a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/faq?categoryid=85B0516024DB47B8B38087911E1BAE6C" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Frequently asked questions (FAQ) | MOE</a></strong> for
enquiries on FAS.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong><u>What to Expect</u></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>&nbsp; Parents will receive an email acknowledgement from MOE HQ upon
submission of the eFAS application.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>FAS processing time takes 3 – 4 weeks.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents who received an email update from MOE HQ on the approval of their
FAS should wait for a follow-up notification from the school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The school will contact parents via PG to inform them of their application
outcome and provide details on FAS collection and benefits (if applicable).</p>
</li>
</ul>
